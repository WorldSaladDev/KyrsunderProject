About Kyrsunder & Estara

Kyrsunder is the name given to this universe and the ancient event that shaped it:

"At the dawn of existence, when the cosmos was a roiling storm of senseless chaos, the primordial beings known as the Wills wove a sigil of incomprehensible scale, a cosmic pattern formed by galactic filaments, the spin of particles, and the flow of time. This spell, the Kyrsunder, stilled the endlessly shifting cosmos, stripped the Wills of their power, and established the physical laws that govern all of existence."

Among its countless worlds, Estara is of special interest. As an Earth-like planet home to intelligent bipedal organisms rather similar to humans, Estara makes the perfect starting point to explore Kyrsunder, and is the focal point of many of the stories told here.
